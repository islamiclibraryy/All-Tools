About Tools

File Transfer: Securely transfer files directly between two devices, no matter where they are. Just follow the 3-step code exchange process to create a direct connection.

QR Code Generator: Easily create QR codes for website links (URLs) or any custom text. You can optionally add your logo in the middle. Download the final QR code image to share or print.

Audio Converter: Change your audio or video files (MP4, M4A, WAV) into the popular MP3 format directly in your browser. Choose the desired MP3 quality (bitrate). The conversion happens right here, so bigger files might take a moment.

How to Connect:

Device 1 (Sender): Click 'Create Code', copy the generated text.

Device 2 (Receiver): Paste Sender's text into 'Paste Code Here', click 'Submit'. Copy the new text generated.

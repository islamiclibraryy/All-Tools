About Webp Moder

Built by a developer who was tired of slow image tools. Designed for speed, privacy, and simplicity.

Who We Are

A Tool Born From Frustration

Webp Moder by Shekh started with a simple problem: converting images to WebP was either too complicated, too slow, or required uploading your private files to some unknown server.

As a developer, Shekh needed a free WebP converter that was fast, private, worked offline, and didn't require signing up for yet another web account. None of the existing tools ticked all those boxes. So he built one.

Webp Moder was launched as a completely free, browser-based image conversion tool with a focus on privacy, performance, and user experience. It's been growing ever since, serving developers, designers, bloggers, and content creators worldwide.

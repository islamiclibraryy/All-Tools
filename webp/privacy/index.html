2. Image Data — Your Files Are Private

This is the most important section of our privacy policy. Our free WebP converter tool operates entirely within your web browser using the HTML5 Canvas API and JavaScript. This means:

  • Your images are never uploaded to our servers
  • Your images are never stored anywhere outside your device
  • Your images are never transmitted over the internet
  • Your images are never viewed by us or any third party

When you close the browser tab or navigate away, all image data is immediately discarded from memory. We have zero access to any files you process through our tool.
